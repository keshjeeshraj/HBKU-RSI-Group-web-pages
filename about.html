Mathematical Optimisation

Our research highlights the importance of optimising the economic and environmental performance of the energy, water and food nexus; problems are tackled using stochastic, heuristic and game-theoretic approaches.

Computational Intelligence

Our research implements supervised and unsupervised learning, probabilistic graphs and time series analysis on weather data and satellite imagery to model the complex interactions between environmental parameters.

Systems
Resilience

Our research considers scenarios of systems being exposed to the dynamic nature of risks and uncertainties, and hence resilience thinking is adopted to model globally coordinated, adaptive and robust inter-networked systems.
